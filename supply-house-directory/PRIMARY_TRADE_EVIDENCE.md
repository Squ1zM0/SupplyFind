## Classification Methodology

For each multi-trade branch, we analyzed:

1. **Official Branch Name**
   - Presence of trade-specific terms (e.g., "Plumbing Supply", "HVAC Supply")
   - Order of trades mentioned (first typically indicates primary)

2. **Official Website URLs**
   - Ferguson store URLs include department codes:
     - `/plumbingpvf-XXXX` = Plumbing primary
     - `/hvac-XXXX` = HVAC primary

3. **Branch Type**
   - "Showroom" or "Home" = Typically plumbing-focused (fixtures/appliances)
   - "Supply" = Check name for trade specification

4. **Business Specialization**
   - Rampart: Boiler/hydronic heating specialist = HVAC
   - Gateway: Identified as "plumbing wholesaler" = Plumbing
   - Winsupply divisions: Winair = HVAC, Winnelson = Plumbing

5. **Notes and Documentation**
   - Existing notes about business focus
   - Parts carried (order and emphasis)
